Drawing Page

Here are some of my pen and ink drawings, many of which are animal and nature imagery. I have an illustrative style. I like to draw Pokemon and cartoon character people. Many of my best drawings come from Inktober! I participate in the yearly drawing challenge on Instagram where there is a drawing prompt for each day of the month in October. It is a fun way to practice art, promote your work, and find other cool artwork online.
